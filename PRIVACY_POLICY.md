# Privacy Policy

**StreakForge**
*Last Updated: February 4, 2026*

---

## Overview

StreakForge ("the App") is developed by RF Animation LLC ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our habit tracking application.

**The short version:** Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or share your personal information or habit data.

---

## Information Collection

### What We Do NOT Collect

- **Personal Information**: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.
- **Habit Data**: We do not collect, access, or store any information about your habits, actions, behaviors, or streaks.
- **Usage Analytics**: We do not track how you use the app, what features you access, or how often you open the app.
- **Location Data**: We do not access or collect your location.
- **Device Identifiers**: We do not collect device IDs, advertising identifiers, or fingerprinting data.

### What We May Receive

- **Crash Reports**: If you opt in to share crash data with Apple, we may receive anonymized crash reports through Apple's App Store Connect. These reports contain no personal information or habit data—only technical information about app crashes to help us fix bugs.

---

## Data Storage

### Local Storage

All your habit data (actions, behaviors, logs, and streaks) is stored **locally on your device**. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it or enable iCloud sync.

### iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync:
- Your data syncs between your own Apple devices using Apple's CloudKit service
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
- Only you can access your data through your iCloud account
- We have no access to your iCloud data
- You can disable sync at any time in Settings

### Data Export

You can export your data at any time as a CSV file. Exported files are saved to a location you choose and are in human-readable format. **Note:** Export files are not encrypted—store them securely if privacy is a concern.

## Data Security

While we don't collect your data, we take the following measures to protect data stored on your device:
- Data is stored using Apple's secure Core Data framework
- iCloud sync uses Apple's encrypted CloudKit service
- No data is transmitted to our servers

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:
- **Access**: View all your data directly in the app
- **Export**: Export your complete data history at any time
- **Delete**: Delete individual items or all data from Settings
- **Portability**: Export data in standard CSV format for use elsewhere
